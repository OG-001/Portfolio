About Me

Building AI-Powered Frontends

I'm a frontend engineer specialising in React, TypeScript, and microfrontend architecture. I designed and built the entire frontend of Ragbrain, a production AI platform, as the sole frontend engineer, and delivered key features on MultiChat LLM, a collaborative enterprise project built on Onyx.

I also architected PHC-Track, an offline-first PWA for Primary Health Centres across Nnewi North LGA, Anambra State, building all 10 modules from patient registration to NHMIS reporting, with NDPA 2023 compliance and full offline capability for low-end Android devices. Beyond product work, I've built and shipped company websites for AI startups including ogesoft.com and insightmesh-ai.com.

My technical range spans Webpack Module Federation, Single-SPA orchestration, real-time LLM streaming, offline-first PWA design with Dexie and service workers, NestJS APIs, RAG pipeline UIs, Next.js App Router, SWR, and Playwright E2E testing.
