PHC-Track

Offline-first Progressive Web App for Primary Health Centres across Nnewi North LGA, Anambra State. Patient registration, maternal care (ANC/PNC), immunization tracking, queue management, and NHMIS-aligned reporting. All fully functional with no internet connection, built for low-end Android devices in underserved communities.

10
Modules Built
10
PHCs Covered

Offline-First
  • Offline-first architecture: Dexie (IndexedDB) + service worker sync engine, installable PWA on low-end Android
  • React + TypeScript + Vite frontend; NestJS REST API; PostgreSQL, Dockerized and self-hosted in Nigeria for data residency
  • 10 modules: Patient Registration, EMR, Maternal Health (ANC/PNC), Immunization (NPI schedule), Queue Management, Staff RBAC, SMS Notifications, Offline Sync Engine, NHMIS Reporting, Admin Dashboard
  • NDPA 2023 compliant: all patient data stays in Nigeria; DHIS2-exportable monthly NHMIS summaries
  • Provider-agnostic SMS abstraction: Africa's Talking + Termii adapters for Nigerian mobile networks
